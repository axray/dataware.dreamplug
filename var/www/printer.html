Print Server

  • The Wireless Sheevaplug can act as a print server over a printer connected through the USB port.
  • A demo page which guides you through the steps to print out a sample page is available here: printer.cgi
  • Most of the common Ubuntu-supported printers are supported.
  • Connect the printer to the USB port of the Wireless Sheevaplug.
  • Now follow the link mentioned above to view the printer demo.
