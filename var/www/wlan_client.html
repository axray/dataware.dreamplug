WLAN Client

  • The Wireless Sheevaplug can act as a wireless client and you can associate with nearby Access Points.
  • Note that this functionality is available only in WLAN Client mode.
  • You can switch into the WLAN Client mode from here: mode-switch.cgi
  • A demo page which guides you through the steps can be accessed by clicking here: wlan_client.cgi
  • You can scan the neighbouring area for Available Wireless Networks and then associate with any one of them.
