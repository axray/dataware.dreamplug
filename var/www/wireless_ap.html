Wireless Router

  • The Wireless SheevaPlug can function as a wireless router device.
  • When powered on, it comes up with a pre-configured SSID 'SheevaPlug-uAP-0001'
  • You will be able to see this SSID in the list of 'Available Wireless Networks' on your wireless client.
  • Clicking on the above SSID will associate your wireless client with the SheevaPlug.
  • Once associated, the wireless client will get an IP address from the range 192.168.1.100 to 192.168.1.200
  • At this point, you will be able to view this page by entering 'http://192.168.1.1/' into the the address bar of the browser running on your wireless client. You may also be able to ssh into the SheevaPlug by specifying the IP address 192.168.1.1
  • If you wish to change the IP address range, please follow the instructions here
  • You will also be able to access other devices connected to the SheevaPlug over wireless, as well as the USB.
  • In addition, if the ethernet port of the SheevaPlug is connected to the internet, you will be able to browse the same.
  • Please note that the wireless network is Unsecured (meaning no password is required for association).
