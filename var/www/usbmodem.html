3G Connectivity Through USBModem

  • You can connect through a 3G USBModem to the internet cloud using Wireless Sheevaplug..
  • Connect your 3G USBModem into the USB port of the Wireless Sheevaplug.
  • A demo page which guides you through the steps can be accessed by clicking here: usbmodem.cgi
  • Support for a bunch of commonly used service providers is available.
  • Examples of Service providers are - Cingular(USA), China Mobile, TataIndiCom etc.
    The detailed list can be seen on the above mentioned page
