Bluetooth Device

  • As an example for bluetooth,you can associate your bluetooth enabled headphones with the plug.
  • After association, a sample wav/mp3 file on the plug can be streamed on to the headphones.
  • For playing the music files please execute following command after the association is complete - 
    aplay -Dplug:headset myfilename.wav
